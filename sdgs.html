Landing Page Website About SDGs

An informative website built to raise awareness about the Sustainable Development Goals.

Project Background

This project was developed as a team-based landing page to provide a clear and concise introduction to the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The website covers key goals such as ending poverty and hunger, ensuring health and quality education, and promoting decent work and economic growth, presented in a visually engaging format.
